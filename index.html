About me

I’m a graduate of Mathematics, but tech has always been my passion. I love desing products that solve user needs, be it websites. web apps or mobile apps.

I’m passionate about design. I design user-centered digital solutions for young startups and established businesses. I have worked with a series of exciting companies and amazing clients. Some of them include Google, Microsoft and Paystack. I’m currently exploring freelance opportunities.

My design tool of choice is Figma, but I’m proficient in the use of Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. Other tools I use for my work are Whimsical, InVision, and Blender.
